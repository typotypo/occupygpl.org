Permissive Licenses

Some popular Permissive licenses.

MIT License

A permissive license that is short and to the point. It lets people do anything with your code with proper attribution and without warranty. License | TLDR; Legal

BSD 2-Clause License

A permissive license lets people do anything with your code with proper attribution and without warranty. License | TLDR; Legal

BSD 3-Clause License

A permissive license lets people do anything with your code with proper attribution and without warranty. With a Trademark clause. License | TLDR; Legal

ISC License

The ISC license is functionally equivalent to the BSD 2-Clause and MIT licenses, removing some language that is no longer necessary. License | TLDR; Legal

Apache v2

A permissive license that also provides an express grant of patent rights from contributors to users. License | TLDR; Legal
